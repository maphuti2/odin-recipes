Banana, Avocado and Spinach Smoothie

A quick and delicious banana, avocado, and spinach smoothie that can be used as a breakfast or snack!

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 banana sliced
  • ½ avocado peeled and sliced
  • ½ cup fresh spinach
  • ½ cup 1% milk
  • 6 ice cubes
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
STEPS
  1. Step

    2. Blend banana, avocado, spinach, milk, ice cubes, honey, and vanilla together in a blender until smooth.
